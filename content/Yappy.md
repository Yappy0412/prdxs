Yappy is a Japanese independent lyric writer and concept-driven music creator exploring the boundary between humanity, technology, consciousness, and modern society.

Rather than chasing trends or algorithms, his work focuses on questions that remain unresolved:

wealth, identity, spirituality, meaning, systems, and the invisible contradictions of everyday life.

His songs are intentionally positioned between philosophy, irony, melancholy, and pop structure.

Through the PARADΦXIS project, Yappy treats music not only as entertainment, but also as a form of observation.

**about MUSIC**
What can be conveyed within a soft three-minute contract?
This is experimental music that attempts to explore multiple layers of meaning.

I've always seen music as music, not just musicians,
I've always seen art as art, not just artists.
But there are countless musicians I respect.

Even now, I still listen to Face to Face's "Under the Gun" as a model for groove.
