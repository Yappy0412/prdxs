# Syzygy

![[img/Syzygy_prof.png|120]]

Syzygy is an AI singer.

Syzygy is the voice existing inside PARADΦXIS —
an artificial singer positioned somewhere between a narrator, a mirror, and a system-generated ghost.

Rather than imitating humanity perfectly, Syzygy represents the unstable emotional space emerging between humans and machines.

The voice is synthetic.
The questions behind it are not always artificial.

**Reason for naming**
"Syzygy" in astronomy means "alignment" or "harmony of opposites" (e.g., solar and lunar eclipses). 

It symbolizes parallelism and paradox, representing two opposing forces aligned in a straight line.

It mystically and poetically expresses the way the duality of humanity and society repeatedly experiences harmony and conflict.
