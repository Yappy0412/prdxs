[Intro]
Do you still want
to be richer tomorrow?

[Verse 1]
Ancient wealth, 
treasures and gold, 
Overflowing palaces, 
needing rooms to hold

Numbers keep rising, 
a warehouse of light, 
Across your ledger lines, 
no ceiling in sight

Overseas flights, 
internet dreams, 
A smartphone in your hand— 
Where even loneliness is traded

[Chorus 1]
Is infinity a miracle?
Wealth is finite,
Calculation infinite.

Is infinity a curse?
What is it
that fills the distance first?

[Verse 2]
The wealth that remains, 
memories and joy,
Infinite creations 
from endless ways combined.

Even digital theft 
can never erase the data,
That treasure inside you 
remains forever yours.

Icarus wings, 
teleporting through,
Telepathy begins 
within envisioned worlds anew.

[Chorus 2]
Is infinity a miracle?
Resources finite,
Calculation infinite.

Is infinity a curse?
The cycle slowly
Breaks and burns.

[Bridge]
It’s a magic box 
people call a bank,
A bottomless warehouse 
without a final rank.

And if we can obtain 
more and more without a stop,
Would we keep reaching upward 
until everything is lost?

[Build-up]
Since ancient days, 
the inner vault remains,
Beneath the lid of Pandora’s box.
Might the key to harmony
Still lie buried there?

[Drop]
"That place has always lived inside you"

[Chorus 3]
Is infinity a miracle?
This planet is finite,
Calculation infinite.

Is infinity a curse?
Debts keep rising,
Layer after layer in the night.

[Final Chorus]
Is infinity a miracle?
Wealth is finite,
Calculation infinite.

Is infinity a curse?
Now it stretches out
Even toward the human heart.

[Cinematic Spoken Outro]
What is
your inner wealth?
