This is how the world appears to me.
A map—
more like a quiet monologue.

It is neither accurate nor complete.
It points to no places, no directions.

Every lyric I write
begins from this map.

It could be a single puzzle piece,
or it could be an entrance.

What lies at the root of it all?
The accumulated trace of questioning,
of continued observation—
I call that the Meta Matrix.
